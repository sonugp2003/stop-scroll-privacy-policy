Privacy Policy for Unscroll

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Quick Summary: Unscroll helps you stay focused by automatically detecting and blocking short-form video content (YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, etc.). We use Google AdMob for ads, which collects your Advertising ID only. All your usage statistics stay on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Unscroll uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. The ONLY data collected is:

  • Advertising ID - A unique identifier used by Google AdMob for showing personalized ads

We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Location data (approximate or precise)
  • ❌ Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, Serial Number)
  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Browsing history or app activity outside Unscroll
  • ❌ Contact information
  • ❌ Camera, microphone, or photos

2. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is NEVER transmitted to our servers:

  • Blocking statistics - Count of how many times short-form content was blocked per app
  • App preferences - Which apps you want to block (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Snapchat, Twitter)
  • Settings - Schedule preferences, theme selection, temporary pause state
  • Biometric lock preference - Whether you enabled fingerprint/face unlock for settings (biometric data never leaves your device)

Note: We have no servers - all your personal data stays 100% private on your device.

3. How We Use Information

Information collected is used for:

  • Ad personalization - Google AdMob uses data to show relevant ads
  • App functionality - To remember your settings and track your progress
  • App improvement - Anonymous crash reports help us fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

Unscroll integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - Advertising and analytics
    Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing - For in-app purchases (premium features)
    Privacy Policy: Google Payments Privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data is shared only with:

  • Google AdMob - For advertising purposes as described in their privacy policy
  • Google Play - For handling in-app purchases

6. Children's Privacy

Unscroll is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data transmitted to AdMob is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Local data on your device is protected by Android's app sandboxing
  • We recommend using device encryption and screen lock for additional security

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Reset advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • Delete local data: Simply uninstall the app - all local data is automatically deleted
  • Disable monitoring: Toggle off app monitoring in Unscroll settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Showing an in-app notification for significant changes

Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Permissions Explained

Unscroll requests the following permissions:

  • Accessibility Service - Required to detect short-form video content in 6 specific apps (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Snapchat, Twitter) and automatically navigate away. This permission monitors ONLY these 6 apps and does NOT record screen content, collect data, or track activity outside targeted apps.
  • Receive Boot Completed - Allows the app to restart the accessibility service after device reboot (if you had it enabled)
  • Internet - Required to load advertisements from Google AdMob
  • Biometric (optional) - To lock app settings with fingerprint or face unlock for additional security

Note: Unscroll does NOT request or use location, camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, or phone permissions.

11. Accessibility Service Transparency

Unscroll uses Android's AccessibilityService API solely for content blocking. Here's exactly what it does:

What It Monitors:

  • Only these 6 apps: YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Snapchat, Twitter/X
  • Detects UI patterns indicating short-form video screens (Shorts, Reels, etc.)
  • No monitoring outside these 6 apps

What It Does:

  • When short-form content is detected, automatically presses the back button or navigates to home screen
  • Updates local blocking statistics (stored only on your device)
  • Nothing else - no recording, no data collection, no transmission

What It Does NOT Do:

  • ❌ Does NOT record or capture screen content
  • ❌ Does NOT collect or read text, passwords, or personal information
  • ❌ Does NOT transmit any data to external servers
  • ❌ Does NOT track your activity outside the 6 targeted apps
  • ❌ Does NOT access clipboard, keyboard input, or other apps

You are in control: You can disable the accessibility service anytime in Android Settings → Accessibility → Unscroll → Toggle OFF.
