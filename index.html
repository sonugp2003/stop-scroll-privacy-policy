Privacy Policy for Unscroll

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Quick Summary: Unscroll helps you stay focused by monitoring your app usage and sending reminders. We use Google AdMob for ads, which collects advertising data. All your usage statistics stay on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Unscroll uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID - A unique identifier for advertising purposes
  • Device information - Device model, operating system version
  • Network information - IP address, connection type
  • Ad interaction data - Which ads you view or click
  • Approximate location - Only if you grant location permissions (not required by our app)

2. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is NEVER transmitted to our servers:

  • App usage statistics (how many times you've opened monitored apps)
  • Your app settings and preferences
  • Biometric authentication settings (if you enable biometric lock)
  • Which apps you've chosen to monitor

Note: We have no servers - all your personal usage data stays private on your device.

3. How We Use Information

Information collected is used for:

  • Ad personalization - Google AdMob uses data to show relevant ads
  • App functionality - To remember your settings and track your progress
  • App improvement - Anonymous crash reports help us fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

Unscroll integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - Advertising and analytics
    Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing - For in-app purchases (premium features)
    Privacy Policy: Google Payments Privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data is shared only with:

  • Google AdMob - For advertising purposes as described in their privacy policy
  • Google Play - For handling in-app purchases

6. Children's Privacy

Unscroll is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data transmitted to AdMob is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Local data on your device is protected by Android's app sandboxing
  • We recommend using device encryption and screen lock for additional security

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Reset advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • Delete local data: Simply uninstall the app - all local data is automatically deleted
  • Disable monitoring: Toggle off app monitoring in Unscroll settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Showing an in-app notification for significant changes

Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Permissions Explained

Unscroll requests the following permissions:

  • Usage Access - Required to monitor which apps you're using and send reminders
  • Notifications - To send you reminders when you're on distracting apps
  • Internet - To load advertisements
  • Biometric (optional) - To lock settings with fingerprint/face unlock
